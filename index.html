Meet Linda Mona,
Your Guide to Wholeness

Linda Mona is a gifted traditional healer and massage therapist born from the rich spiritual heritage of the Eastern Cape. Her healing touch is not merely technique — it is a divine calling, passed through generations and refined through decades of dedicated practice.

At Songo Healing Sanctuary, Linda creates a sacred container where your body, mind, and spirit are met with deep reverence. Each session is uniquely attuned to your energy, your story, and your need for restoration.
